Ceviche

Description:

An iconic dish made with fresh fish or seafood marinated in lemon juice, red onion, chili, cilantro, and salt.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pinch of pepper
  • 2 Pounds of fish of your choice
  • 1 Onion cut into julienne strips
  • Â¼ Cup chopped cilantro leaves
  • 1 Chopped yellow pepper
  • 1 Chopped limo chili (for decoration)
  • 12 Lemons
  • 1 Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 Cup of fish broth

To accompany:

  • 1 Boiled tender corn
  • 1 Boiled sweet potato
  • 1 Bunch of lettuce leaves

Steps:

  • 1. Prepare the tiger's milk:
  1. In the blender, place the juice of 1-2 lemons, some fish cubes, a piece of onion, a piece of limo chili, a few cilantro stalks and, optionally, the garlic.
  2. Blend for a few seconds.
  3. Strain the mixture and reserve the "tiger's milk" in the refrigerator.
  • 2. Marinate the fish:
  1. Place the cut fish in a glass or stainless steel bowl.
  2. Season the fish with salt to taste and add the chopped limo chili. Stir gently.
  • 3. Integrate the flavors:
  1. Pour the remaining lemon juice and reserved "tiger's milk" over the fish.
  2. Add the chopped cilantro and mix with a spoon. The marinating time is very short in authentic Peruvian ceviche, so the fish doesn't overcook and retains its freshness.
  3. Adjust the salt if necessary.
  • 4. Add the onion:
  1. Add the julienned red onion, previously washed.
  2. Mix one last time gently.
  • 5. Serve:
  1. Serve the ceviche immediately in deep bowls to keep the juices cool.
  2. Accompany each dish with sweet potato and corn.
  3. Sprinkle the popcorn on top or serve it on the side for a crunchy touch.
  4. The remaining liquid in the dish, the "tiger's milk," is also a fundamental part of the experience.
