Pollo a la Brasa

Description:

Pollo a la brasa is a specialty of Peruvian cuisine. It consists of a whole chicken, marinated in a mixture of herbs and spices, slowly cooked in a charcoal or wood-fired oven. The result is crispy, golden skin and incredibly juicy and tender meat with a distinctive smoky flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken: A whole chicken, which is marinated and cooked completely.
  • Garlic: Several cloves of garlic, finely minced or crushed, to provide an intense and aromatic flavor.
  • Soy sauce (sillao): Adds a salty and umami touch, and also helps with the coloring of the skin.
  • Vinegar or lemon: Adds acidity and helps to tenderize the meat.
  • Cumin: A fundamental spice that gives the marinade a warm and earthy flavor.
  • Black pepper: Ground or whole, for a spicy and aromatic touch.
  • Oregano: Preferably dried, for a classic herbaceous flavor.
  • Paprika: Adds color and a slightly sweet or smoky flavor, depending on the type.
  • Ají panca or ají amarillo: Peruvian chili pastes that add a unique flavor and a characteristic, subtle heat. It is a key ingredient for the traditional flavor.
  • Vegetable or olive oil: Helps the spices adhere better to the chicken and contributes to crispy skin.
  • Salt: Essential for seasoning and enhancing all the other flavors.

Steps:

  1. Prepare the marinade: Blend ají panca or ají amarillo paste, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, cumin, oregano, pepper, paprika, and salt until smooth.
  2. Marinate the chicken: Coat the whole chicken with the blended mixture, making sure it's fully covered. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours, or ideally overnight, for the best flavor.

3. Cook:

  1. Oven method: Preheat the oven to a high temperature (around 450°F / 230°C). Roast for about 15 minutes, then reduce the heat to 325°F / 160°C and cook until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown.
  2. Grill method: Use medium-high indirect heat on the grill, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  3. Rest and serve: Let the chicken rest for 15 minutes before carving to keep it juicy. Serve with fries and salad.
